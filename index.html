Deep Learning UFC Fight Predictor

How to use this tool: Select two fighters and choose a date for each fighter. At the bottom of the page, press the "Calculate" button. This will apply deep learning algorithms to analyze all known fighter stats (scraped from ufcstats.com) and predict the most likely outcome if they were to fight exactly as they were on the specified dates. Details on how these models were constructed can be found here on our Github. Accuracy of predicting the correct winner is 64%. All photos are scraped from Google image search acted on by a convolutional neural network (CNN) for artistic style transfer (8,964 photos total).
